## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Owned Brands Presentation
The system SHALL have a dedicated section for products manufactured by the company, starting with the "Beneva" brand.

#### Scenario: Client browses owned industry products
- **WHEN** a user navigates to the "Indústria" page
- **THEN** they are presented with the Beneva brand identity and its product line

### Requirement: Beneva Product Catalog
The system SHALL display the initial Beneva product portfolio, specifically: mussarela barra, coalho barra, and provolone bastão.

#### Scenario: Client checks Beneva catalog
- **WHEN** a user views the Beneva brand section
- **THEN** they see detailed information and appealing imagery for "mussarela barra", "coalho barra", and "provolone bastão"
