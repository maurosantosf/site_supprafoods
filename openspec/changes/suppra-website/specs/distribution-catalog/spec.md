## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Distributed Brands Portfolio
The system SHALL display a clear and organized catalog or listing of all partner brands distributed by Suppra Foods.

#### Scenario: Retailer searches for represented brands
- **WHEN** a user visits the "Distribuição" page
- **THEN** they see a categorized visual list of partner brands available for purchase

### Requirement: B2B Practical Usability
The system SHALL prioritize a highly practical interface for the distribution section, allowing retailers and supermarkets to quickly find products and request contact.

#### Scenario: Supermarket buyer wants to become a client
- **WHEN** a B2B buyer finds a brand they want to purchase
- **THEN** they have an immediate, obvious call-to-action to contact a commercial representative or request a catalog
