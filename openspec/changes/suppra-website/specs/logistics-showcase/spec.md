## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Logistics Operations Showcase
The system SHALL display detailed information about the company's logistics infrastructure, focusing on storage capacity, cold chain, and expedition efficiency.

#### Scenario: Industry partner evaluates logistics
- **WHEN** a user visits the "Operador Logístico" page
- **THEN** they see high-quality images of the warehouse, metrics on storage capacity, and details about the fleet

### Requirement: Strategic Location Highlight
The system SHALL prominently feature the strategic location of the distribution center (saída para Trindade, antiga Creme Mel).

#### Scenario: Partner checks geographic advantage
- **WHEN** a user reads about the logistics operation
- **THEN** they are informed about the strategic headquarters location and its logistical benefits for distribution
